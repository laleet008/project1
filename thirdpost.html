Manchester City wins 20th straight game to lead Premier League by 13 points

Agency 25 Feb 2021

Luis Suarez has been a revelation since swapping Barcelona for Atletico Madrid and Atleti fans will be hoping to see his famous celebration on consistent basis this season.

He has since fired in the goals and helped Diego Simeone’s men to the top of the table. He’ll face Chelsea in the Champions League last-16 with Blues fans remembering the Uruguayan well from his time at Liverpool. Suarez will now doubt be desperate to get on the scoresheet and wheel away in celebration in his trademark fashion. But what does his celebration mean?



He has a tattoo on his wrist that reads ‘Delfina’, which is the name of Suarez’s eldest daughter. First he kisses his ring finger as a nod to his wife Sofia, before kissing the tattoo. And finally he completes the celebration by kissing three of his fingers to signify his wife and two kids.
